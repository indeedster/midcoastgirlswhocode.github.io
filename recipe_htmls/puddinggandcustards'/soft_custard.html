Soft Custard

Ingredients


2 cups milk or cream
3 eggs or 6 egg yolks, lightly beaten
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla or 1/2 tsp grated orange or lemon peel

Instructions

1) Scald milk in top of double boiler
2) Combine eggs, sugar, and salt in bowl. Gradually add scalded milk
3) Return to double boiler. Cook over hot, not boiling water, stirring constantly until mixture coats a metal spoon
4) Cut into squares when cool.
